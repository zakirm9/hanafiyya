About Us

The Al Hanafiyya server is an Islamic Server based on the Hanafi Madhab, where you can talk with other Muslims, learn about the religion, participate in lessons, and read from our Library! There is a welcoming community, and members from all the different Madhabs inside. The Staff are respectful and active, and keep the chat clean from bad actors!

We have:

  • • IslamQA
  • • Huge Islamic Library
  • • Fun Events
  • • Minecraft Servers
  • • Engaged Muslim Community
  • • Lessons
  • • Quran Listening Sessions
  • • Our own websites

and much more.

We have an abundance of books pertaining to Hanafi Fiqh (jurisprudence), Usool 'al-Fiqh (Fundamentals of Jurisprudence), Tafseer (Quranic exegesis), Tajweed (Elocution), Hadith (Sayings of the Prophet ﷺ ), Arabic, and Seerah (Life of the Prophet ﷺ ). As well as this, we have biographies of the key scholars of the Hanafi Madhab, like Imām Abū Hanīfa, Imām Abū Yūsuf, and Imām Muḥammad. To top it off, there is a Ḥanafi Fiqh section which shows rulings of different issues that a Muslim comes across in his everyday life, like Wuḍū, Ghusl, and Salāh. For the benefit of the members of this server, we recently introduced a Homework channel where people can ask for help regarding any work they have, and a Questions channel too!

So what are you waiting for? Go ahead and join!
